  2. Tetora is an earnest, hard-working guy. His ultimate goal is to become a “man among men,” which ends up being much less clear-cut as Tetora’s journey of self-discovery continues. His idea of what a “manly man” is often influenced and shaped by his idolization of Kuro. The traits Tetora tends to point out as being manly often include physical strength, cool-headedness, determination, as well as honesty and ability to care for others. He notably values certain skills that are traditionally associated with femininity, such as cooking and sewing.
